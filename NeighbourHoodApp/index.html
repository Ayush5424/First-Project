About Us

NeighborAid is a website designed to connect community members who need help with daily tasks, such as grocery shopping, prescription pickups, and errands, with volunteers willing to assist. The website fosters local engagement by providing a simple platform for neighbors to support each other, ensuring that elderly individuals and those with mobility issues receive the help they need. By leveraging technology, NeighborAid enhances convenience, strengthens community bonds, and promotes a culture of kindness and mutual aid.
